About EGMS Data

The European Ground Motion Service (EGMS) provides consistent, reliable information about natural and anthropogenic ground movement across Europe, derived from Sentinel-1 SAR data.

Data Types

  • L2ABasic level — SAR geometry parameters
  • L2BIntermediate — calibrated SAR geometry
  • L3Advanced — geographic coords, 100 × 100 km tiles

L2A / L2B Parameters

  • Relative OrbitSAR orbit number (e.g. 052)
  • Burst CycleTiming cycle ID (e.g. 0716)
  • SwathIW1 / IW2 / IW3
  • PolarizationVV / VH / HH / HV

L3 Parameters

  • E / N Tiles100 km × 100 km ETRS89-LAEA grid
  • E componentEast–West displacement
  • U componentVertical (Up–Down) displacement
